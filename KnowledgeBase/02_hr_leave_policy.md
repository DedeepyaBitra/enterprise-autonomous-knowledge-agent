# NovaTech Solutions — HR & Leave Policy

All leave balances reset on **January 1st** each year (calendar-year cycle). Leave is credited monthly on a pro-rata basis (i.e., 1/12th of annual entitlement credited each month), except for new joiners, who receive pro-rata credit from date of joining.

## 1. Annual Leave (AL)

- Entitlement: **18 days/year** (1.5 days/month accrual).
- Can be availed in blocks with minimum 2 days' advance notice; leave of 5+ consecutive days requires 7 days' advance notice.
- Maximum carry-forward to next year: **10 days**. Remaining unused AL beyond 10 days lapses on December 31st unless local labor law requires encashment.
- Encashment on exit: up to 15 accumulated days, at basic pay rate.

## 2. Sick Leave (SL)

- Entitlement: **12 days/year** (1 day/month accrual).
- Leave exceeding 2 consecutive days requires a medical certificate uploaded to the HRMS portal.
- Sick leave cannot be carried forward; unused SL lapses at year-end (not encashable).
- SL may be availed same-day with morning notification to manager before 10:00 AM.

## 3. Casual Leave (CL)

- Entitlement: **6 days/year** (0.5 day/month accrual).
- Used for short personal matters (max 2 consecutive days per request).
- Cannot be combined with Annual Leave in a single request without HRBP approval.
- Non-encashable, non-carry-forward.

## 4. Emergency Leave

- Not a separate leave bucket — drawn from Casual Leave or Annual Leave balance, marked as "Emergency" in HRMS for tracking.
- Applies to genuine unforeseen circumstances (family medical emergency, accident, bereavement).
- May be availed **without prior approval**, provided the employee or their emergency contact notifies the manager within **24 hours** of leave commencement.
- Bereavement leave (immediate family: parent, spouse, child, sibling) grants an additional **5 days of paid leave**, separate from AL/CL/SL buckets, requiring no advance notice.

## 5. Work From Home (WFH) Policy

- Hybrid employees may avail up to **8 WFH days per month**, requested via HRMS at least 1 day in advance (same-day WFH permitted for genuine reasons with manager approval via Slack/email).
- WFH days require availability during core collaboration hours (11:00 AM–4:00 PM IST) and standard check-in/check-out logging.
- WFH is not counted against leave balance.
- Repeated unapproved WFH or unavailability during WFH may result in WFH privileges being suspended by the manager, with HR notification.

## 6. Remote Work Policy

- Fully remote designation is role-based and requires approval from a VP or above at time of hiring or via a formal request reviewed by HR + department head.
- Remote employees follow the same core collaboration hours and attendance logging as hybrid employees.
- Remote employees are eligible for Internet Reimbursement (see Expense & Software Access Policy).
- Remote employees are required to visit the nearest NovaTech office at least once per quarter for team syncs, travel reimbursed as per Travel Reimbursement policy.

## 7. Holiday Policy

- NovaTech observes **12 paid public holidays per year**, published annually in January via HRMS calendar, based on regional/national holidays applicable to the employee's registered work location (Bangalore, Pune, or remote-location equivalent).
- 2 of the 12 holidays are **floating holidays**, employee-selectable from a list of regional/festival options.
- If a public holiday falls on a weekend, no substitute weekday is granted unless declared by HR in the annual calendar.

## 8. Leave Approval Workflow

1. Employee submits leave request via HRMS portal, specifying leave type, dates, and reason.
2. Direct manager receives notification and must **approve or reject within 48 hours**.
3. If no action is taken within 48 hours, the request **auto-escalates to the skip-level manager**.
4. Approved leave automatically updates the employee's leave balance and shared team calendar.
5. Leave requests exceeding **10 consecutive days** additionally require HRBP notification (informational, not approval-blocking).
6. Retroactive leave requests (for sick/emergency leave already taken) must be filed within 3 business days of return.

## 9. Attendance Rules

- Employees must check in and check out daily via HRMS (biometric in-office, geotagged/self-service for WFH).
- Missing check-in/check-out for 3+ days in a month without correction requests may result in those days being marked Loss of Pay (LOP), pending manager confirmation.
- Attendance regularization requests (forgot to check in/out) must be submitted within 5 days with manager approval.

## 10. Late Coming Policy

- Grace period: **15 minutes** past scheduled start time (9:30 AM), no action taken.
- Beyond grace period, late arrival is logged.
- **3 late arrivals in a calendar month** are permitted without penalty.
- From the **4th late arrival** in the same month onward, each instance results in a **0.5 day LOP deduction** unless regularized with a valid, manager-approved reason (traffic emergencies, medical appointments, official travel).
- Persistent late-coming patterns (5+ instances for 3 consecutive months) trigger an HR conversation and may factor into performance review.

## 11. Salary Cycle

- Salary is processed **monthly**, for the calendar month worked, credited between the **1st and 3rd of the following month**.
- Payslips are published on the HRMS portal by the 5th of each month.
- Salary revisions (promotions, increments) are effective from the 1st of the month following approval, unless otherwise specified in the promotion letter.

## 12. Notice Period

- **During Probation (first 6 months):** 15 days' notice, either party.
- **Post-confirmation:** 60 days' notice, either party.
- Notice period may be bought out (by employee) with HR and manager approval, at gross salary rate for the unserved portion.
- NovaTech may place an employee on **Garden Leave** during notice period at its discretion, with full pay, releasing them from active duties but retaining employment status.
- Immediate termination for gross misconduct forfeits standard notice period provisions.

## 13. Promotion Policy

- Promotions are considered during the **bi-annual review cycle** (April and October), aligned with the Performance Review Process.
- Eligibility: minimum 12 months in current role, "Exceeds Expectations" or sustained "Meets Expectations" ratings, manager nomination.
- Promotion decisions require manager recommendation + department head approval + HRBP calibration review (to ensure cross-team consistency).
- Off-cycle promotions are permitted only for exceptional impact, requiring VP-level approval and HR calibration sign-off.
- Promoted employees receive a minimum salary increment as defined by the applicable compensation band (managed by Finance/HR Compensation Committee, not published externally).
