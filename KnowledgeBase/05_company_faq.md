# NovaTech Solutions — Company FAQ (109 Questions)

## General & Company

**1. What does NovaTech Solutions do?**
NovaTech Solutions is an enterprise AI software company building AI workflow automation products, including autonomous agents, RAG-based enterprise search, and CRM/GTM automation tools.

**2. Where is NovaTech headquartered?**
Bangalore, India, with a secondary office in Pune and a distributed remote workforce.

**3. What are NovaTech's core values?**
Customer Obsession, Ownership, Transparent Communication, Bias for Action, and Respect & Inclusion.

**4. What are the standard office timings?**
9:30 AM to 6:30 PM, Monday to Friday, including a 1-hour lunch break.

**5. What are core collaboration hours?**
11:00 AM to 4:00 PM IST — all employees, including remote and WFH, must be available during this window.

**6. Is NovaTech hybrid, remote, or office-based?**
Hybrid is the default model (minimum 3 office days/week). Fully remote roles exist but require VP-level approval.

**7. How do I report a security issue?**
Email security@novatechsolutions.com immediately and cc your manager. Major incidents also auto-escalate to L3 IT/Security leadership.

**8. What is NovaTech's disciplinary process?**
A three-stage process: Verbal Warning → Written Warning → Termination, except gross misconduct (fraud, harassment, data theft), which can lead to immediate termination.

**9. Where are company announcements posted?**
Via company-wide email and the #announcements Slack channel.

**10. What tools does NovaTech use for internal communication?**
Slack (real-time), Email (formal/external), Jira (tasks), and Confluence (documentation).

## HR & Leave

**11. How do I apply for leave?**
Submit a leave request via the HRMS portal specifying leave type, dates, and reason. Your manager must approve or reject within 48 hours.

**12. How many Annual Leave days do I get?**
18 days per year, accrued at 1.5 days/month, with a maximum carry-forward of 10 days into the next year.

**13. How many Sick Leave days do I get?**
12 days per year, accrued at 1 day/month. Not carried forward or encashable.

**14. How many Casual Leave days do I get?**
6 days per year, accrued at 0.5 day/month, max 2 consecutive days per request.

**15. Do I need a medical certificate for sick leave?**
Only if you're taking more than 2 consecutive sick days — upload it to the HRMS portal.

**16. What happens if my leave request isn't approved within 48 hours?**
It auto-escalates to your skip-level manager for approval.

**17. Can I take emergency leave without prior approval?**
Yes — for genuine emergencies, notify your manager (or have someone notify them) within 24 hours of the leave starting.

**18. Is there a separate emergency leave balance?**
No — emergency leave draws from your Casual or Annual Leave balance but is tagged "Emergency" in HRMS.

**19. What is the bereavement leave policy?**
5 additional paid days for immediate family bereavement, separate from your AL/CL/SL balances, with no advance notice required.

**20. Can I work from home?**
Yes, hybrid employees can take up to 8 WFH days per month, requested at least 1 day in advance via HRMS.

**21. Does WFH count against my leave balance?**
No, WFH days are not deducted from leave.

**22. How do I become a fully remote employee?**
Remote designation requires VP-level approval at hiring time, or a formal request reviewed by HR and your department head.

**23. Do remote employees need to visit the office?**
Yes, at least once per quarter for team syncs, with travel reimbursed.

**24. How many public holidays does NovaTech observe?**
12 per year, including 2 floating holidays you can choose from a list of regional/festival options.

**25. What happens if a holiday falls on a weekend?**
No substitute weekday is granted unless HR specifically declares one in the annual calendar.

**26. What's the grace period for late arrival?**
15 minutes past 9:30 AM.

**27. What happens if I'm late more than 3 times in a month?**
From the 4th late arrival onward in the same month, each instance results in a 0.5 day LOP deduction, unless regularized with a valid manager-approved reason.

**28. How do I regularize a missed check-in/check-out?**
Submit a regularization request in HRMS within 5 days, with manager approval.

**29. When is salary credited?**
Between the 1st and 3rd of the following month, for the month worked.

**30. When are payslips available?**
By the 5th of each month, on the HRMS portal.

**31. What is the notice period during probation?**
15 days, either party.

**32. What is the notice period after confirmation?**
60 days, either party.

**33. Can I buy out my notice period?**
Yes, with HR and manager approval, at gross salary rate for the unserved portion.

**34. What is Garden Leave?**
A discretionary arrangement where NovaTech relieves you of active duties during notice period while continuing full pay and employment status.

**35. How often are promotions considered?**
Bi-annually, during the April and October review cycles.

**36. What are the eligibility criteria for promotion?**
Minimum 12 months in current role, "Exceeds Expectations" or sustained "Meets Expectations" ratings, and manager nomination.

**37. Can I get an off-cycle promotion?**
Only for exceptional impact, requiring VP-level approval and HR calibration sign-off.

**38. How is my probation period structured?**
6 months, with reviews at Day 90 and Day 180.

**39. Can I carry forward unused Casual Leave?**
No, Casual Leave is non-carry-forward and non-encashable.

**40. What happens to unused Annual Leave when I resign?**
Up to 15 accumulated AL days are encashed at basic pay rate as part of your Full & Final settlement.

**41. Can I combine Casual Leave and Annual Leave in one request?**
Not without HRBP approval.

**42. What if I need to file a sick leave request after I've already returned?**
Retroactive leave requests must be filed within 3 business days of your return.

**43. Is there a limit to how many WFH days I can take in a row?**
The monthly cap is 8 WFH days; extended stretches should be discussed with your manager to ensure core-hour availability.

**44. Who approves leave exceeding 10 consecutive days?**
Your manager approves it, and HRBP is notified for informational purposes (not approval-blocking).

## IT Support

**45. How do I reset my password?**
Use the self-service portal at passwordreset.novatechsolutions.internal with OTP verification — takes under 5 minutes.

**46. What if self-service password reset fails?**
Raise a P1 ticket via the IT Helpdesk Portal; IT will verify your identity and manually reset within 30 minutes.

**47. How often do I need to change my password?**
Every 90 days, meeting complexity requirements (12+ characters, uppercase, lowercase, number, special character).

**48. My VPN isn't connecting — what should I do?**
Update your VPN client via Software Center and restart your device. If it persists, raise a P2 ticket (target resolution: 4 hours).

**49. Is VPN required for remote work?**
Yes, VPN is mandatory to access internal tools like Jira, Confluence, and internal repos while remote or WFH.

**50. My email isn't syncing — what do I do?**
Try re-authenticating your mail client first. If unresolved, raise a P2 ticket (target: 4 hours).

**51. What's my mailbox storage limit?**
50GB, with automated alerts at 45GB.

**52. My laptop is running slow — what should I do?**
Run the NovaTech Diagnostics tool from Software Center, which auto-reports to IT. This is treated as a P3 ticket (target: 4 hours).

**53. How do I install new software?**
Approved software is self-installable via Software Center. Non-listed software requires an IT Helpdesk request with manager approval, plus Security approval for elevated-permission tools.

**54. How long does software installation approval take?**
1 business day once manager (and Security, if needed) approvals are complete.

**55. What if the office internet is down?**
Check #it-announcements for status updates; this is centrally monitored and logged as a P3 ticket if unresolved.

**56. My home internet is unreliable for WFH — can IT help?**
IT can issue a mobile hotspot for critical outage periods; ongoing home internet costs are covered separately under Internet Reimbursement.

**57. My printer shows offline — what do I do?**
Check the printer status dashboard first, then try reinstalling drivers via Software Center. Unresolved issues are logged as P3 tickets (1 business day).

**58. My account is locked — what happened?**
Accounts lock after 5 failed login attempts. They auto-unlock after 30 minutes, or you can raise a P1 ticket for immediate manual unlock.

**59. What happens if my account gets locked repeatedly?**
3+ lockouts in a month trigger a mandatory security awareness check-in with IT Security.

**60. My laptop hardware is faulty — what's the process?**
IT diagnostic confirms the fault, a replacement request is auto-created, and a loaner device is issued within 24 hours while replacement is arranged (target: 3-5 business days).

**61. What's the laptop refresh cycle?**
3 years for standard company laptops.

**62. Is accidental damage to my laptop covered?**
Yes, the first accidental damage incident is covered by company asset insurance; repeat damage within 12 months may be partially charged to the employee.

**63. How do I escalate an IT ticket?**
Reply "escalate" on your ticket or email itescalations@novatechsolutions.com. SLA breaches auto-escalate from L1 to L2, and further to L3.

**64. What's the difference between P1 and P3 tickets?**
P1 (Critical) targets 2-hour resolution for complete work stoppage; P3 (Medium) targets 1 business day for partial functionality issues.

**65. Who do I contact for IT issues?**
The IT Helpdesk Portal or itsupport@novatechsolutions.com; urgent issues can also go to #it-urgent on Slack.

**66. What triggers an automatic major incident notification?**
System-wide outages or security breaches automatically notify the CTO and all L3 staff, regardless of manual escalation.

**67. Can I install personal software on my company laptop?**
Only if it's in the approved Software Center list, or approved via an Access Request with manager (and Security, if needed) sign-off.

**68. How quickly will IT respond to a VPN certificate expiry?**
This is treated as a P2 issue, targeting 4-hour resolution.

**69. What should I do before submitting a laptop troubleshooting ticket?**
Run the NovaTech Diagnostics tool from Software Center first — this often resolves common issues and provides IT with diagnostic data.

## Expense & Software Access

**70. What's the food reimbursement limit during travel?**
₹500/day domestic, ₹1,500/day international, with receipts required above ₹200 per meal.

**71. Can I get food reimbursement for a regular office day?**
No, food reimbursement only applies during official business travel.

**72. What's the internet reimbursement for WFH employees?**
₹1,000/month, for employees on WFH 4+ days/month or fully Remote, submitted quarterly with a broadband bill.

**73. How much can I claim for certifications?**
Up to ₹20,000/year, with mandatory pre-approval from your manager and the L&D team before enrollment.

**74. What if I resign shortly after a certification reimbursement?**
If you resign within 6 months of receiving reimbursement over ₹10,000, that amount is deducted from your Full & Final settlement.

**75. How do I submit an expense claim?**
Via the Expense Portal, within 15 days of incurring the expense, with itemized receipts attached.

**76. What's the reimbursement approval threshold for Finance sign-off?**
Any claim above ₹10,000 requires Finance approval in addition to manager approval.

**77. When do I get reimbursed after my claim is approved?**
In the next payroll cycle following approval.

**78. What happens if my expense claim is rejected?**
It's returned with a reason and can be resubmitted with corrections within 7 days.

**79. Is there a deadline to submit expenses?**
Yes, 30 days from the date incurred, except with an HR-approved exception.

**80. What class of flight can I book for domestic travel?**
Economy class, booked via the approved corporate travel partner where possible.

**81. Can I book premium economy for international travel?**
Only for Director+ level on flights exceeding 6 hours, with VP approval.

**82. What's the accommodation cap for domestic travel?**
₹8,000/night in metro cities, ₹5,000/night in non-metro cities.

**83. Do I need approval before booking travel?**
Yes, all travel must be pre-approved by your manager via the Travel Request Form before booking.

**84. How do I request GitHub access?**
Submit a request via the IT Helpdesk Portal's Access Request category; it requires manager approval plus Engineering Lead (repo owner) approval.

**85. What GitHub access level do I get by default?**
Write access to your team's repositories; org-wide Admin access is restricted to Engineering Managers and above.

**86. How do interns/contractors get GitHub access?**
Time-boxed, read-only access by default, which auto-expires at contract end date.

**87. How do I request Figma access?**
Viewer access requires no approval at all and is broadly available to Product, Engineering, and Marketing. Editor access requires manager approval, plus Design team lead approval for shared design systems/libraries, and is restricted to Design and Product roles.

**88. Do I need approval for basic Jira access?**
No, standard project-level Jira access is auto-provisioned during onboarding based on your department.

**89. How do I get access to a Jira project outside my team?**
Requires your manager's approval plus the target project's Jira Admin approval.

**90. Who can get Jira Admin access?**
Engineering Managers, Product Leads, and IT Operations only.

**91. How do I request OpenAI API access?**
Via the IT Helpdesk Portal, requiring approval from your manager, then Department Head, then mandatory Security & Compliance sign-off due to data sensitivity.

**92. Can I use my own OpenAI API key for work projects?**
No, all usage must go through NovaTech's approved internal proxy gateway with logging and PII redaction; direct unproxied key usage is prohibited.

**93. Is there a usage cap on OpenAI API access?**
Yes, usage is capped by your team's assigned budget tier; overages require Finance approval.

**94. How often is software access reviewed?**
Quarterly by IT Security; access unused for 60+ days is automatically flagged for revocation.

**95. What's the general approval chain for software access requests?**
Direct Manager approval → Tool Owner/Admin approval → IT provisions access, on a least-privilege, role-based basis.

## Onboarding, Offboarding & Performance

**105. What happens on my first day?**
HR orientation, handbook walkthrough, policy acknowledgment sign-off, and buddy assignment.

**106. How do I resign from NovaTech?**
Submit your resignation in writing via the HRMS portal; your notice period depends on whether you're on probation (15 days) or confirmed (60 days).

**107. What happens to my software access when I resign?**
IT Operations initiates an access revocation checklist during offboarding — email, VPN, GitHub, Jira, Figma, and OpenAI API access are all removed as part of clearance.

**108. When is my Full & Final settlement paid?**
Within 45 days of your last working day, including leave encashment (up to 15 AL days), reimbursements, and applicable deductions.

**109. How often are performance reviews conducted?**
Bi-annually — H1 (April–September) and H2 (October–March) — including self-assessment, manager assessment, and 360 peer feedback for Senior+ roles.
